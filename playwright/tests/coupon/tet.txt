Bây giờ dùng lệnh này:

  Chạy bình thường có browser:
  npx playwright test tests/coupon/admin-coupon-ui.spec.ts --headed

  Chạy chậm để demo (800ms mỗi bước):
  $env:SLOWMO=800; npx playwright test tests/coupon/admin-coupon-ui.spec.ts --headed

  Chạy 1 browser thôi cho gọn khi demo:
  $env:SLOWMO=800; npx playwright test tests/coupon/admin-coupon-ui.spec.ts --headed
  --project=chromium
